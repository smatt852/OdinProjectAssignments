THE ODIN PROJECT

It is a free, comprehensive curriculum that equips learners to be full stack developers, whatever their experience level. Topics include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Accessibility, GitHub and Programming Logic. It is open source and community driven. It emphasizes practice and learning by doing. Learning happens while building projects; a few have been provided here as examples.

To Do List App

A useful app to help you be productive. Put action items in a list and set the urgency level and deadline. Classify them into categories and view by category. Take a quick look at what's due today.

Battleship Game

The classic game of Battleship. Build your fleet on the grid and hide them, then pass your device to your opponent and hope they can't find them. When the fleet is sunk, see how many shots it took and try to beat it.

Photo Slideshow

Organise your pics into a little slideshow. You can scroll forward or backward at your own pace, or let the app do it for you. Pause on a good shot whenever you want.

Knight's Path in Chess

Knights can cover the whole chess board. Pick your start and end positions, and the app will show you how the knight can get there in the least moves.

Rock Paper Scissors

Another classic game. Try to beat the computer with your best choices. Win three games to win the match.

TicTacToe

Play the computer to win this time-tested game.
